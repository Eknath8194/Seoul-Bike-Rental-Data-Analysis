# 🚲 Seoul Bike Rental Data Analysis

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes the **Seoul Bike Rental dataset** to understand how time, weather conditions, and seasonal factors affect bike rental demand. The goal is to identify useful patterns that can help improve operational planning and decision-making.

## 🎯 Business Objective

To analyze rental demand patterns and support **data-driven decisions** for better bike availability, staffing, and maintenance planning.

## 📊 Dataset Features

The dataset includes hourly rental information along with environmental and operational factors:

* Date & Hour
* Rented Bike Count
* Temperature
* Humidity
* Wind Speed
* Rainfall & Snowfall
* Seasons
* Holiday
* Functioning Day

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn

## 📈 Analysis & Visualizations

The following visualizations were used to explore the dataset:

* **Bar Charts** – Seasonal rental comparisons
* **Pie Charts** – Category distribution
* **Countplots** – Frequency of categorical variables
* **Line Charts** – Rental trends over time
* **Histograms** – Distribution of numerical variables
* **Heatmap** – Correlation between features
* **Box Plots** – Outlier detection
* **Scatter Plots** – Relationship between variables

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Rental demand varies significantly by **season and time of day**.
* **Weather conditions** such as temperature and rainfall impact bike usage.
* **Holidays and operational days** influence rental patterns.
* Environmental factors show noticeable **correlation with rental demand**.

## 💼 Business Impact

The insights from this analysis help in:

* Better **demand forecasting**
* Optimizing **bike allocation**
* Improving **staffing and maintenance planning**
* Enhancing **customer experience**

## 🏁 Conclusion

The analysis reveals clear demand patterns influenced by environmental and time-based factors. Understanding these patterns helps organizations improve operational efficiency and make informed business decisions.
